About Us

Hamari Naz Foundation (HNF) is a family-sponsored charitable foundation, registered in 2025 under the Companies Act 2017. Inspired by the values of generosity, diversity, and service embodied by Shamshaz Naz Shaikh, the Foundation was established by her children and grandchildren to honor her legacy.

Mission

To empower individuals and communities through education, healthcare, skills development, and social welfare.

Core Values

  • Compassion: Acting with empathy and care
  • Integrity: Upholding transparency and accountability
  • Sustainability: Creating solutions that endure
  • Empowerment: Enabling self-reliance and dignity

Our Objectives

  1. Provide essential healthcare services and medical support through free camps, preventive care, and consultations.
  2. Promote education by offering scholarships, training programs, and mentoring opportunities.
  3. Support families with food supplies, groceries, and basic necessities.
  4. Assist underprivileged families with marriage support and women’s empowerment programs.
  5. Facilitate access to clean water through filtration plants and dispensers.
